I'm an artist based in the DC area. Otherwise known as the DMV or Nova since I live in Northern Virginia. And it's the aftermath
of said space anomaly. It's a black hole that I've seen destroy the lives of too many people. And I'll be leaving here pretty soon.
This is one of the most affluent areas in the country, but somehow I missed out on all of that. I took a different path
and paid the price. Most of these pieces of art reflect that price and paint the picture of my misguided decisions combined with unfortunate luck.
I do, however, appreciate all art and don't really like to stick to one genre. Some are nice and friendly and some are dark yet honest.
Some are landscapes. Some are portraits. Some are imaginative constructs of my personal experiences, combining genres and techniques to form
something entirely new. I prefer the latter. I prefer expression and creativity rather than just drawing what I see with my physical eyes.
Welcome to my mind.
